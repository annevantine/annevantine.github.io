Local Orchards

 There are five apple orchards within a few miles of each other in the small town of Stow, Massachusetts:

 
Carver Hill Orchard
Route 62 West
Brookside Ave.
Stow, MA 01775
Tel: 978-897-6117
 Old-fashioned, scenic farm offering Pick-Your-Own apples. Also grows peaches and vegetables. Carver has a very large cider-pressing operation.
Derby's Orchard
Route 117
Stow, MA 01775		 Derby's store is open year round offering apples, dried flower arrangements and  home-pressed cider.  They offer pick-your-own at their Maynard orchards.
Honey-Pot Hill
Orchards
144 Sudbury Rd.
Stow, MA 01775
Tel: 978-562-5666
www.honeypothill.com		 Has 25 variety apple orchard/cider press, 
petting zoo, picnic area and hayrides on  weekends. 
Hosts school tours and company outings.
One Stack Farm
Route 117
Stow, MA 01775
www.onestack.com 		One-Stack has an open shed with a refrigerator full of cider, and a card table with a sign saying to take your cider and leave your money.  Behind the house are the orchards.
Shelburne Farm
106 West Acton Rd.
Stow, MA 01775
Tel: 978-897-9287
www.shelburnefarm.com		 Offers Pick-Your-Own apples, peaches and pumpkins. 
Makes its own jams and jellies. 
Picnic area and miniature apple trees for kids.
